Frequently asked questions

How do I sync my data between devices?
The app uses iCloud to sync automatically. Make sure iCloud is enabled on all your devices (Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud) and that the app has iCloud access. Your data will appear on all devices signed into the same Apple ID.
I reached the 5-item limit. How do I add more?
The free version supports up to 5 tracked items. Tap the "Unlock Unlimited" button in the app to purchase a one-time upgrade — no subscription required. Your purchase applies to all devices linked to your Apple ID.
How do I restore my purchase on a new device?
Go to Settings within the app and tap "Restore Purchases." The app will check your Apple ID for previous purchases and unlock automatically.
Can I export my data?
Yes. Go to Settings and choose "Export as CSV" or "Export as PDF." You can share the exported file via email, AirDrop, Files, or any other sharing method.
How does the receipt scanner work?
When you add an item and take a photo of a receipt, the app uses on-device text recognition (OCR) to read the date, store name, and amount. All processing happens on your device — nothing is sent to any server.
How do I delete my data?
You can delete individual items by swiping left in the All Items list or by tapping "Delete Item" in the item detail view. To remove all data, uninstall the app. To also remove iCloud data, go to Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud > Manage Storage on your device.
Why am I not getting reminder notifications?
Make sure notifications are enabled for the app in your device Settings > Notifications > Covered. Also verify that the notification toggles (30 days, 7 days, 1 day) are turned on for each item you want reminders for.
Does the app work offline?
Yes. All features work without an internet connection. iCloud sync will resume automatically when you're back online.
