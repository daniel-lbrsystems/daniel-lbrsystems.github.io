Privacy Policy

Warranty Tracker & Reminders — by LBR Systems LLC
Last updated: March 24, 2026

The short version

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. Your information stays on your device and in your personal iCloud account. That's it.

Data storage

All data you enter into the app — item names, dates, store names, amounts, notes, and receipt photos — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. If you have iCloud enabled, your data syncs to your personal iCloud account via Apple's CloudKit. This sync is between your own devices only. We have no access to your iCloud data.

Data we collect

None. We do not collect analytics, usage data, crash reports, device identifiers, location data, or any other personal information. The app contains no analytics SDKs, no tracking pixels, and no third-party code that collects data.

Receipt photos and OCR

When you photograph a receipt, the image is processed entirely on your device using Apple's Vision framework for text recognition (OCR). No images or extracted text are sent to any server. Receipt photos are stored in your iCloud container alongside your other app data.

Advertising

The app contains no advertisements and no advertising SDKs.

In-app purchases

The app offers optional in-app purchases processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not receive or store any payment information. Apple handles all transaction processing. See Apple's Privacy Policy for details on how Apple processes purchases.

Third-party services

The app does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs. The only external service used is Apple's CloudKit for iCloud sync, which is governed by Apple's privacy policy and your iCloud settings.

Notifications

The app can send local notifications to remind you of upcoming expiration dates. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device and do not involve any server communication. You can manage notification permissions in your device's Settings.

Children's privacy

The app does not collect data from any user, including children. There are no accounts, no registration, and no data transmission.

Data deletion

Since all data is stored locally and in your personal iCloud, you have full control over your data at all times. You can delete individual items within the app, or delete all app data by uninstalling the app. To remove iCloud data, go to Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud > Manage Storage on your device.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change our privacy practices, we will update this page. Since we don't collect any data, we don't anticipate changes, but we'll keep this page current regardless.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at apps@lbrsystems.com.
